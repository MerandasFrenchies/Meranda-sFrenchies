Breeding exceptional Mini French Bulldogs with love and care

About Us

Welcome to Luxury French Bulldogs, where we take pride in raising healthy and happy Mini French Bulldogs. Unlike traditional breeders, our dogs are raised as beloved pets, living inside our home and surrounded by family. We do not kennel our dogs, and both the mother and father are on-site and available to meet.

Meet the Parents

Our breeding pair are exceptional examples of Mini French Bulldogs:

  • Olive: A compact 14 lb brindle beauty with a gentle and loving temperament.
  • Benson: A striking 20 lb lilac and tan male, known for his playful and affectionate nature.

Both Olive and Benson are AKC registered and free of any health or genetic issues.
